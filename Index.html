Celebrating the bond I shared with my dogs, Buster and Winston.

My Favorite Breed

I love all dogs, but my favorite breed is the Border Collie. Their intelligence, loyalty, and energy make them exceptional companions.

Childhood Memories

Growing Up with Buster and Winston

From age 8 until I left for college, I grew up with two incredible dogs: Buster, a Border Collie-Australian Shepherd mix, and Winston, an adorable but slightly overweight Basset Hound. I would take them on walks every day, where Buster would lead the way, and Winston, with his short legs, would follow behind. Walking them brought them so much joy, and their excitement was contagious!

Living in the countryside, we walked on trails through the woods. They roamed freely without leashes, enjoying the fresh air and exploring while never straying far from me. These walks were some of the happiest moments of my childhood.

Hide and Seek with Buster

Buster and I shared a special game of hide and seek. I would throw a toy down the hall, and while she fetched it, I would hide. She’d come back with the toy and realize I wasn’t there, then search for me using her sharp sense of smell. Once she found me, we’d both be thrilled and continue the game. It’s a memory that still makes me smile today.

The Bond We Share with Dogs

Dogs bring unconditional love, loyalty, and comfort. Whether it's providing emotional support or simply being there to greet you at the end of the day, they’re always ready to make us feel valued and happy. My dogs were truly my best friends, and their companionship is something I will always cherish.
